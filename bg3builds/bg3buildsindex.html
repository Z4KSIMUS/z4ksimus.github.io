Index of Optimized Baldur's Gate 3 Builds

The following builds:

  • Optimize specific fantasies/concepts. Some of these builds are outclassed by other builds that just don't capture the right fantasy.
  • Realize their concept early, within the first ~5 levels. Everything after that should just make the build stronger.
  • Can perform in something close to a vacuum:
    • Don't rely on specific allies external to the build itself to realize their concept (with the exception of support builds), just make it better.
    • Don't rely on any specific items, be that unique magic items, consumables in general, or bonuses from making specific story decisions to realize their concept, just makes it better.
  • Assume Honour Mode. These builds will all obviously perform spectacularly on Tactician and below, but won't be maximally optimal because Honour adds four major considerations:
    • Extra actions granted through Hastened or Elixir of Bloodlust do not benefit from Extra Attack (Action Surge still benefits from Extra Attack).
    • Pact of the Blade's Deepened Pact does not stack with Extra Attack to reach 3 attacks/action at level 8.
    • Damage Rider Sources instead act as ordinary damage riders.
    • These builds place higher value on options that provide consistency to success, over options that make you succeed harder, which is valuable without the ability to reload saves.
